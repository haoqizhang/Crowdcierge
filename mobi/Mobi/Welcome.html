HARVARD TOUR

Imagine a Harvard visit for prospective students and families that is entirely planned by students like you, who know just what is cool, fun and interesting about this place called Harvard.


Mobi is a new way for large numbers of people to plan events together through micro-contributions. We are in the process of testing Mobi, and would like to invite you to plan a Harvard tour together with other Harvard students. You will be able to add new ideas for activities, plan out the itinerary, and leave notes for others - and do all this in less than 5 minutes. We expect this to be a lot of fun, and will even throw in $50 Amazon gift cards for two randomly drawn participants!
